Subject: Requirements for E-commerce Platform
From: stakeholder@company.com
Date: Jan 15, 2024

Hi team,

After our meeting, here are the key requirements for the new e-commerce platform:

1. User Authentication: The system must provide secure user authentication with multi-factor authentication support. This is critical for security.

2. Product Catalog: Users should be able to browse products with advanced filtering options including price range, category, and ratings.

3. Shopping Cart: The platform needs to support a persistent shopping cart that saves items even when users log out.

4. Payment Integration: We require integration with multiple payment gateways including PayPal, Stripe, and credit cards.

5. Performance: The application must load pages in under 2 seconds and support at least 10,000 concurrent users.

6. Mobile Support: The platform should be fully responsive and provide a native-like experience on mobile devices.

Please review and let me know if anything is missing.
